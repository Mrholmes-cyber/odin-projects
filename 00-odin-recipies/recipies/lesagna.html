Descrition

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of layered sheets of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy b√©chamel or ricotta, and melted cheese.

Baked until bubbly and golden on top, it combines savory flavors and comforting textures in every bite. Often served as a hearty main course, lasagna is known for its warmth, richness, and ability to bring people together around the table.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • ground meat

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and cook the lasagna noodles.
  2. Prepare the sauce with meat and tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat the layers until ingredients are used.
  5. Bake in the oven until hot and bubbly.
