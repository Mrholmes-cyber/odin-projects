Pasta

Pasta, sauce, cheese

Description

Pasta is a classic Italian dish made from wheat-based noodles. It is commonly served with tomato, cream, or pesto sauces. Pasta is loved worldwide for its simplicity and comfort.

Ingredients

  • pasta noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. Heat the sauce.
  3. Drain the pasta.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce.
  5. Serve with cheese.
