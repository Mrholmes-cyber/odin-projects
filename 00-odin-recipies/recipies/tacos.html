Tacos

Tortillas, meat, vegetables

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made with soft or crispy tortillas filled with a variety of ingredients. They are often packed with seasoned meat, fresh vegetables, and flavorful sauces. Tacos are popular for their bold flavors and versatility.

Ingredients

  • tortillas
  • ground beef or chicken
  • lettuce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the meat with seasoning.
  2. Warm the tortillas.
  3. Add meat to the tortillas.
  4. Top with lettuce and cheese.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
