Sushi

Rice, fish, seaweed

Description

Sushi is a Japanese dish made with vinegared rice and a variety of fillings. It often includes raw fish, vegetables, and seaweed. Sushi is known for its fresh taste and careful presentation.

Ingredients

  • sushi rice
  • raw fish
  • nori (seaweed)
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook and season the rice.
  2. Prepare the fish and vegetables.
  3. Place rice and fillings on nori.
  4. Roll tightly.
  5. Cut into pieces and serve.
